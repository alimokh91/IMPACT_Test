subroutine for computing the difference coefficients for derivatives of order ABL in direction DIR.

Parameters
[in]dirdirection of the derivative
[in]ablorder of derivative, mapping only supported with abl<=2
[in]upwind{-1,0,1}
[in]mapping_yesif true, stencils are computed on equidistant grid, then mapped to physical grid
[in]dim_ncbsize of coefficient stencil ?
[in]n_coeff_boundnumber of coefficents in stencil ?
[in]nmaxlocal max gridpoint number
[in]bl?
[in]bu?
[in]xccoefficient coordinates (onto where value are transfered)
[in]xecoordinates of transferred value
[in]bclBC at lower limit
[in]bcuBC at upper limit
[in]grid_typetype of grid 0: pressure, 1: u, 2: v, 3: w ?
[out]cccoefficients
